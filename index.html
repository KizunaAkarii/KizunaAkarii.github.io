Avatar

This page gives you a basic introduction of the greatest movie in history.

Avatar is a science fiction film directed by James Cameron and acted as a screenwriter, produced by Fox in the 20th century, and starred by Sam Worthington, Zoe Soldana and Sigourney Weaver. It was released in North America on December 16, 2009 in 2D, 3D and IMAX-3D formats.

This film tells the story that in the future, in order to obtain the resources of Pandora planet, humans started the Avatar Plan, and the local Na'vi people fought with humans to protect their homes.

The film was released on December 16, 2009. It will be shown again in mainland China on March 12, 2021.
